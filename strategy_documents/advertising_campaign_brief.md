# Rental Village: Summer Advertising Campaign Brief

## 1. Campaign Overview & Strategic Alignment

**Objective:** Capitalize on the peak summer season (July-September) by launching a targeted Facebook and Instagram advertising campaign. This campaign will promote bundled equipment rentals for popular summer projects, positioning Rental Village as the go-to resource for homeowners and contractors in the Carleton Place area.

**Key Goals:**
*   Drive short-term (weekend and multi-day) rentals for high-margin equipment.
*   Increase brand awareness and engagement within our target demographics.
*   Promote the economic and sustainable benefits of renting over buying.
*   Track and measure ROI through concrete metrics like promo code usage and website conversions.

## 2. Target Audience

*   **Primary Audience: Women Aged 35–55:** Value-conscious decision-makers, often planning family and community-oriented projects.
    *   **Messaging:** Focus on convenience, achieving professional results easily, and creating beautiful outdoor spaces for summer enjoyment.
*   **Secondary Audience: DIY Enthusiasts (Men & Women, 30-60):** Hands-on individuals who need specific tools to complete their ambitious summer projects.
    *   **Messaging:** Focus on the power and precision of our equipment, the availability of pro-grade tools, and the satisfaction of a job well done.

## 3. Summer Product Bundling Strategy

Bundles will be designed to offer clear value and align with common summer projects.

*   **Bundle 1: The Ultimate Deck & Fence Bundle**
    *   **Equipment:** Post Hole Auger, Power Washer, Paint Sprayer.
    *   **Use Case:** Building a new fence or deck, or cleaning and re-staining an existing one.
    *   **Economic Angle:** "Get the tools the pros use for a fraction of the cost. Rent the bundle and save 15%!"

*   **Bundle 2: The Patio Perfect Pack**
    *   **Equipment:** Mini Excavator (Kubota U25), Plate Compactor, Concrete Mixer.
    *   **Use Case:** Installing a new stone patio or walkway.
    *   **Expertise Highlight:** "From foundation to finish, get a flawless patio with our pro-grade bundle."

*   **Bundle 3: The Lawn & Garden Makeover Kit**
    *   **Equipment:** Sod Cutter, Tiller, Lawn Roller.
    *   **Use Case:** Removing old grass, preparing new garden beds, or laying fresh sod.
    *   **Eco-Messaging:** "Transform your green space sustainably. Rent what you need, return it when you're done."

*   **Bundle 4: The Canada Day Party Pack (Limited Time)**
    *   **Equipment:** Large Tent, BBQ, Tables & Chairs.
    *   **Use Case:** Hosting a backyard party for Canada Day or other summer events.
    *   **Convenience Pitch:** "Host the perfect summer party, without the clutter. We deliver and pick up!"

## 4. Facebook & Instagram Carousel Ad Strategy

Carousel ads will be the primary format, showcasing the steps and equipment for each project.

*   **Ad Template 1: "Build Your Summer Oasis" (Deck & Fence Bundle)**
    *   **Card 1:** Post hole auger in action. "Step 1: Set your foundation."
    *   **Card 2:** Power washer cleaning a fence. "Step 2: Refresh and prep."
    *   **Card 3:** Paint sprayer finishing a deck. "Step 3: Add the final touch."
    *   **CTA:** "Rent the Deck & Fence Bundle & Save! ➡️"
    *   **Tracking:** Promo code `SUMMERDECK15`.

*   **Ad Template 2: "Your Perfect Patio, Made Easy" (Patio Pack)**
    *   **Card 1:** Mini excavator digging. "Precision digging for a perfect base."
    *   **Card 2:** Compactor leveling the ground. "A solid foundation is key."
    *   **Card 3:** A beautiful finished patio. "The stunning result."
    *   **CTA:** "Get a Free Quote on Your Project Bundle!"
    *   **Pixel Integration:** Track clicks to the "Earth Moving Equipment" page.

## 5. Conversion Tracking & ROI Measurement

*   **Facebook Pixel:** Install on the website to track page views, reservation form submissions, and custom events.
*   **Promo Codes:** Assign unique, memorable codes to each ad to track offline (phone/in-store) and online conversions.
*   **Call Tracking:** Use a dedicated phone number for ad campaigns to track inquiry calls.

## 6. Budget & Optimization

*   **Initial Daily Ad Spend:** $75-$125, focused on the highest-margin bundles.
*   **A/B Testing:** Test different ad copy, visuals (video vs. image), and CTAs to identify top performers.
*   **Retargeting:** Create a custom audience of users who have engaged with our ads or visited the website but not converted. Serve them a follow-up ad with a compelling offer (e.g., "Still thinking about it? Get 10% off your first rental!").
