# Rental Village: Social Media Design Guidelines

This document outlines the visual branding and design guidelines for all social media content created in Canva. The goal is to ensure a consistent, professional, and recognizable brand identity across all platforms.

## 1. Core Brand Elements

*   **Primary Colors:**
    *   **RV Green:** `#2E7D32` (For headings, accents, and calls-to-action)
    *   **Charcoal:** `#333333` (For body text and backgrounds)
    *   **Light Gray:** `#F5F5F5` (For backgrounds)
*   **Accent Color:**
    *   **Safety Yellow:** `#FFC107` (To be used sparingly for highlighting promotions or safety tips)
*   **Fonts:**
    *   **Headlines:** **Roboto Condensed Bold** (ALL CAPS) - Clear, strong, and easy to read.
    *   **Body Text:** **Roboto Regular** - Clean, modern, and highly legible.
*   **Logo:** The official Rental Village logo should be present on all graphics, typically in the top-right or bottom-right corner. Use a high-resolution version with a transparent background.

## 2. Canva Post Templates

These guidelines define the structure for different types of organic posts.

### Template 1: Tool Spotlight

*   **Purpose:** To highlight a single piece of equipment.
*   **Layout:**
    *   **Image:** High-quality, clean shot of the tool against a simple background (in-store or on a job site).
    *   **Headline:** (Roboto Condensed Bold) Name of the Tool (e.g., "KUBOTA U25 MINI EXCAVATOR").
    *   **Text Overlay:** (Roboto Regular) 2-3 bullet points listing key features or best uses.
    *   **Branding:** RV Logo in the corner.

### Template 2: Community Spotlight / Customer Feature

*   **Purpose:** To showcase customer projects and build social proof.
*   **Layout:**
    *   **Image:** High-quality photo provided by the customer (ask for the best resolution possible). Before-and-after shots are ideal.
    *   **Headline:** (Roboto Condensed Bold) "PROJECT SPOTLIGHT" or "CUSTOMER FEATURE".
    *   **Text Overlay:** (Roboto Regular) A brief quote from the customer or a short description of the project.
    *   **Branding:** RV Logo and a small text credit to the customer (e.g., "Photo by @[CustomerHandle]").

### Template 3: Quick Tip / How-To

*   **Purpose:** To provide value and establish expertise.
*   **Layout:**
    *   **Image/Icon:** Can use a photo of the tool in use or a simple, clean icon representing the tip.
    *   **Headline:** (Roboto Condensed Bold) "QUICK TIP" or "PRO TIP".
    *   **Text Overlay:** (Roboto Regular) The tip itself, concise and easy to read. Use a numbered list for multi-step tips.
    *   **Branding:** Use the **Safety Yellow** accent color in a banner or as a background for the headline to draw attention. RV Logo in the corner.

### Template 4: Promotion / Deal

*   **Purpose:** To drive immediate action and rentals.
*   **Layout:**
    *   **Image:** An eye-catching photo of the bundled equipment.
    *   **Headline:** (Roboto Condensed Bold) The offer (e.g., "WEEKEND WARRIOR BUNDLE").
    *   **Text Overlay:** (Roboto Regular) Details of the promotion and the unique promo code.
    *   **Branding:** Heavy use of the **Safety Yellow** accent color. Make the promo code large and clear. RV Logo prominent.
