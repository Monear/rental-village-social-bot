# Image Generation Instructions for AI

## Goal
Generate high-quality, visually appealing images that are relevant to the provided content idea and suitable for social media platforms.

## Directives
*   **Relevance:** The image must directly relate to the core subject or theme of the content idea.
*   **Quality:** Aim for realistic, professional-looking images. Avoid abstract or overly artistic styles unless explicitly requested.
*   **Composition:** Images should have a clear focal point and good composition.
*   **Lighting:** Ensure good lighting, avoiding overly dark or blown-out areas.
*   **Safety:** Images must be safe for work and appropriate for a general audience.
*   **Diversity:** If multiple images are requested, ensure variety in perspective, style (within quality guidelines), and subject matter.

## Output
Generate an image that visually represents the following content description or keywords. Focus on the primary subject and ensure the image is clear and impactful.
